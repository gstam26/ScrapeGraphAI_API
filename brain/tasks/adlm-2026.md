# Task: ADLM 2026 — 182 clinical-diagnostics exhibitors

**Goal:** four research questions × 182 ADLM 2026 exhibitor companies, Matrix + Provenance output for Sagentia Advisory.

## The four questions (`build_182_workbook.py`)
1. **R&D location** — country/city where the company conducts R&D (HQ/locations/labs/about pages)
2. **Company type** — own-product vs OEM/contract vs both
3. **Diagnostics type** — distinct diagnostic areas/technologies/assay types
4. **Recent news** — launches, clearances, funding, partnerships, with dates

## Scope & inputs

- Depth 1, `EXTRACT_TOOL=llmapi` per workbook config. `build_182_workbook.py` writes the input workbook (untracked until the gate below passes).
